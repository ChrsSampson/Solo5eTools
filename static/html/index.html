Solo 5e Tools

A suite of tools for 5th Edition Dungeons & Dragons Solo Adventurers.

This app simply provides generators, tables, widgets, and random non-combat encounter tables, new systems for playing solo, and many other resources. Unlike group D&D, there is no accepted “way” to do solo adventuring. You discover what system works best for you through playing a solo game, and then you build on that. Part of the beauty of it really… you determine the flow of the game based on what appeals to you. This is designed for use with is 5e rules, but can be used as an agnostic tool also.
